Why Macroshifts

It's the closest thing to a founder seat — without the cap table.

Most studios outsource the senior thinking and hand the work to a junior bench. We do the opposite. You'll plan the product, build it, ship it, and stand next to the founder when it's time to raise. Every quarter, you'll see the result of work you actually did.

In exchange we ask for taste, ownership, and the kind of judgement that only comes from having shipped before. We don't have an "associate" tier. There's no internal politics about whose name goes on the deck. The studio is small on purpose.

Equity in what we ship

A studio-wide carry pool that pays out across every portfolio company we help raise.

2

Senior-only team

No bench, no associates, no shadow billing. The people who plan are the people who build.

3

Remote-first, in real time zones

Hubs in London and Lisbon, fully remote for the rest. We work async with a four-hour overlap.

Learning budget that actually works
