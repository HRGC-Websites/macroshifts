The startup journey

Three stages we meet you at.

Stage 01

Pre-product

You have an idea. We validate, design, and ship a working MVP — fixed scope, fixed fee, 6–10 weeks.

Stage 02

Pre-revenue

Product exists, customers don't. We dial in positioning, run growth experiments, and prep the raise.

Stage 03

Pre-Series A

Revenue and momentum — we scale ops, harden the platform, and route warm introductions to seed/A funds.

Engagement model

Studio Partner — fee plus equity.

Most early-stage founders engage us on a Studio Partner basis: a reduced cash fee plus a small equity stake. We win when you win — which means the team building your product genuinely needs the company to succeed.

For shorter engagements (validation, MVP only, or one specific service), we also run fixed-scope Sprints — quoted per project after a short discovery call.
