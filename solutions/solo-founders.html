How a solo founder engagement works

First call, week-one plan, ship by week six.

  • Day 1. 45-minute call. You describe the idea; we ask the questions that hurt.
  • Day 3. A written one-page plan: scope, timeline, fee, equity, risk register.
  • Day 7. Signed engagement. Senior team assigned. First sprint starts.
  • Week 2–6. Build. Demo every Friday. You learn enough about the product to sell it.
  • Week 7+. Live product. Real customers. We stay for go-to-market and raise; you stay in control.
