What we hear in the first meeting

Four problems showing up in every transformation conversation.

The legacy system is unkillable.

The mainframe / Oracle / SAP install is bolted to twenty business processes. Nobody wants to be the one who broke billing for a week.

Tooling sprawl is out of control.

Forty-three SaaS contracts, eleven of them duplicates. Procurement asks for a rationalisation plan; nobody has time to write one.

Compliance never moves at the same speed as delivery.

SOC 2 audits, GDPR DSARs, and internal risk reviews block release after release. The team starts working around them.

The IT roadmap is twelve months behind the business.

By the time a request gets shipped, the business needs something else. The shadow-IT problem is just the visible symptom.
